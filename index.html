About Me

I'm a passionate digital individual born to a South Sudanese parent and spend most of my life as refugee in Kakuma, Kenya. I completed my primary and high school in the year 2016 and 2020 respectively. I joined Don Bosco Technical Institute coming 2021 where I completed a six-month Computer Application Certificate. This is were my interest in technology begins. In the early 2022, I was lucky enough to be employed as a primary school teacher with the prestigious Lutheran World Federation. In August same year, I joined Xavier Institute of Management(XIM) in coordination with Jesuit Worldwide Learning(JWL) in a four-years degree program of Bachelor of Arts in Sustainable Developer where i was awarded a Certificate in Liberal Studies after the completion of a one year bridging program into the BASD program. I'm currently doing this course of which i expect that by mid 2026, I will be graduated with the Bachelor Degree. In 2024 after gaining more interest in the digital world, I join Action for Refugee Life (AREL), a refugee non-profit organization that offer tech-related courses where i joied them and completed a six-month Certificate in Data Analysis with analysing tools such as Excel, R, SPSS and Tableau. I however goes on and learn PowerBi on my own of which i can used to make effective data driven solutions. I also join them again in Aug 2024 in the then Frontend Development program where we learn a lot of things.
